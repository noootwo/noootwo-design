- `.noootwo/system.md`
- `.noootwo/design-tokens.md`
- `.noootwo/brief.md`
- `.noootwo/style-calibration.md`
- `.noootwo/directions.md`
- `.noootwo/review.md`
- `.noootwo/handoff/`

Keep detailed Noootwo Design workflow rules inside the `$noootwo-design` skill; do not duplicate them here.
